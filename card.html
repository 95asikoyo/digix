Computer Aided Design (CAD)

Computer-Aided Design (CAD), is the use of dedicated computer software to draft, draw and design 2D and 3D objects and structures. Used extensively in the construction,b architecture and design industries, CAD programmes allow users to easily create construction drawings and plans. Professional CAD training and courses usually focus on a specific software, such as Autodesk AutoCAD 2D or 3D, and may be geared towards complete beginners or experienced users looking to gain advanced skills. In either case, a basic level of IT knowledge, usually related to the Microsoft Windows Operating System, is required for an effective learning experience.
